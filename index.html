P-04 Retention

Churn Diagnostics: Who Leaves, When, and Why

Problem

High attrition raising replacement cost. Business needed to know which roles churn first, at what tenure, and why — so retention spend could be targeted, not broadcast.

Who · when · why
Who: Sales and junior roles churn at 2.4× base rate
When: Exit concentrates at 12–18 month tenure — before full productivity
Why: Overtime + low satisfaction = near-certain exit. High-satisfaction overtime workers stayed.

Decision + model

Target retention spend at Sales roles at 12-month mark. Random Forest (ROC-AUC 0.78+) ranks individuals for manager intervention — model comes last, after the diagnosis.

Cohort analysis scikit-learn Random Forest Feature importance
